# AQA GCSE Chemistry (8462) — Curriculum Overview

## Assessment Structure

- **Paper 1** (1h 45m, 100 marks): Topics 1–5
- **Paper 2** (1h 45m, 100 marks): Topics 6–10

Questions in Paper 2 may draw on fundamental concepts and principles from Topics 1–3.
